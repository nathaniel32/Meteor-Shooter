Meteor Shooter Playing Guide
  • Move the cursor towards the incoming meteors.
  • Click the mouse to shoot the meteors.
  • Press the 'E' on the keyboard to enable/disable autoshooter.
    • When automatic shooting is enabled, the meteor speed will increase.
  • Large meteors may require more than one shot.
  • The game will end if a meteor hits you.
